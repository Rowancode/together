Our Vision

The relationships a person has often determine the life they will have.

The key causes of poverty: educational failure, worklessness, family breakdown, debt, and addiction all have relationship breakdown at their core. Equally, the increasingly stressful and unsatisfying lives of some of the most successful can be explained in terms of relationship poverty.

Creating a relationship-centred society is the most important issue we face, and holds the key to solving some of our biggest societal problems. The EU Referendum showed that it’s not just individuals who feel isolated. It revealed a huge divide in the nation; an ‘Un-United’ Kingdom.

Together exists to heal these rifts, both in the lives of individuals and the nation as a whole.

What is Together?

To achieve this Together will launch in three stages:

The Blog – the last couple of months have changed the direction of this country. We are at a crossroads. Together is launching the Blog, a platform for those who are passionate about solving the problems we face and those who are affected by them to discuss the kind of reform we need.

Act Together – words without action are empty. Following the Blog we will launch Act Together to begin to implement this change. Partnering with charities, social enterprises and community action groups we will campaign for change at a national level and support grassroots movements on the ground.

Renewal Together – policy reform and social action only matter if they flow down to everyday life. To create a society characterised by understanding and compassion requires more than policy and action. It requires cultural engagement. To make sure we achieve real and meaningful change Renewal Together will partner with those creating culture across all media.

These sections will work together to enact our vision.
